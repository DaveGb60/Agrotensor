First time using FarmDesk — quick start

  1. Install & open — Download FarmDesk on your device and open the app. No signup required to explore.
  2. Create your farm — Add your farm name and basic details. This creates a local database on your device.
  3. Work offline — Enter livestock, crop, and financial records even without internet. FarmDesk is offline-first and fast.
  4. Back up securely — Create encrypted backups stored locally or to your preferred cloud (end-to-end encryption protects your data).
  5. Sync devices — Connect other devices with a secure pairing code to keep teams in sync. Syncs are encrypted and tamper-evident.
  6. Invite your team — Add users and set roles for the right level of access across the farm.
  7. Get insights — Access financial summaries, productivity reports, and tamper-proof activity logs to support decision-making.
