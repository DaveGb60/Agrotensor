You're Offline

AgroTensor requires the app to be loaded before you can work offline. Once loaded, all your data works without internet.

What you can do: Connect to the internet and reload to load the app, or wait for your connection to come back online.

💡 Tips for working offline:

  • Open the app while online to cache everything
  • All your projects and records are stored on your device
  • You can work completely offline once the app is loaded
  • Changes sync automatically when you're back online
